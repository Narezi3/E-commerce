Problem Statement
E-commerce Data Analysis Power BI project
A US-Based Ecommerce sales company  wants us to create a sales dashboard showing information of YTD Sales and generate
insights  for below scenarios:

1.Create a KPI Banner showing YTD Sales ,YTD Profit,YTD Quantity Sold and YTD Profit Margin
2.Find YOY growth for each KPI.
3.Find the YTD Sales,PYTD Sales,YOY Sales growth for different customer category using a table chart.
4.Find YTD sales performance by each state using a map
5.Top 5 and bottom 5 products  by sales using a bar chart
6.YTD Sales by Region to know best and worst performing region all over the country using a doughnut chart
7.YTD Sales  by Shipping type to get the best shipping type percentage using a doughnut chart

Deliverables
1. Prepare a comprehensive report in a PowerPoint highlighting the Key findings.
2. Explain the business impact of your findings and how they can inform decision-making
3. Propose at least two strategic actions based on your analysis that could help improve business outcomes.
